.. _cloudview:

Cloud View
=========
| Cloud view is opened from the live view screen.
| Cloud view screen shows either *24/7 video logs* or the *motion
  detection recordings*, depends on how the view is opened.
| Both the camera and viewer **should be linked to the same Dropbox
  account**!
|
| |cloud view|
|
Date and time
-------------

| The date and time of the event is shown in the title of the cloud view
  screen.
| Pan the video gallery left and right to search across all videos

Play and pause
--------------

| Press the triangle play button to start the video replay.
| Press the button again to pause the replay.

Delete video(s)
---------------

| Long press a video clip.
| You will see a pop up menu. with two options
- *delete one video*
- *delete all videos*

Developer tips
--------------

1. The video logs are saved in “*\apps\wardencam\log*" folder
2. Motion recordings are saved in “*\apps\wardencam\event*" folder
3. The camera and viewer **should be linked to the same Dropbox
   account!**
